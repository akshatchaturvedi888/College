College of Science and Engineering

Overview

SR Group of Institutions, established in 2002, is an embodiment of advancement in the sphere of engineering education. The institute is approved by All India Council of Technical Education and affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (formerly, Uttar Pradesh Technical University). 

The group offers various courses like B. Tech (CS, EC,EE, ME & CE), B. Pharm, B.Sc. (Hons) Bio-Tech,, BBA, BCA, B.A. Journalism, B.Sc (H) Food Technology, B.Sc Agriculture, MBA (Dual MBA), MCA, M. Tech (CS, EC, ME & CE) & Polytechnic(EE, EC, ME & CE). SRGI is currently running five colleges, College of Science & Engineering, College of Pharmacy, College of Engineering Management & Technology, College of Professional Studies, SR College of Polytechnic. 


All the colleges are well developed with modern infrastructure and advanced laboratories and workshops. The campus is an inspirational place to study, where students are encouraged and supported to reach their highest possible potential through the rich curriculum designed to suit their aspirations and strengths. We maintain a high standard of education and continuously strive to create an ambience for learning in order to promote great career. We believe in the process of reshaping the attitude of students and giving them the opportunity to explore and rediscover themselves. 


We are dedicated to provide state-of-the-art education to enable students to work on teams and have interdisciplinary knowledge, which will cater to industry and social needs. We attempt to provide an imaginative and innovative ethos that combines sound business principles with instinctive commercial drive. Our courses offer students a life changing experience, preparing them to succeed in the ever changing global business arena. As a holistic approach, SRGI students learn to work under highly demanding schedules and perform in the most inspiring way.Your journey starts when you apply for admission and gains momentum as you enroll with the SRGI group.
